Overview

Purpose

This website is meant to generate excitement for a local craft fair. All crafters are from the area and everything will be handmade. Great for that one of a kind Christmas gift you are looking for.

Audience

Who doesn't love Christmas! This fair will have a little bit for everyone. All ages are welcome. Come and see all the creative gifts we have and taste all the wonderful treats that will be for sale.

Content

Home page

Support local artists: Home Page; Main: All the items showcased at our craft fair will be hand made by residents. This is a great opportunity to come out and support our city. Not only would you be showing your support, but you will be able to get a jump on your holiday shopping. Everyone has that person that is hard to shop for. Come get your one-of-a-kind gift for that special person on your Christmas list. Location: Home Page: Main: We will be gathering at the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints on the corner of Litchfield Road and Wigwam Blvd. The address is 301 W Wigwam Blvd, Litchfield Park, AZ 85340. There will be parking at the church and directly across the street is an open lot for back up parking. Make sure to reserve the building and block out Thurs Dec. 1st to get the building ready to host the event. Date and Time: Home Page: Main: Friday Dec. 2nd and Saturday Dec. 3rd. from 9am through 6pm. This is your chance to come out and mingle with neighbors and friends. The first 100 people to come through the door will be entered into a drawing for a 100-dollar shopping voucher you can use with any vendor at the fair.

Images for the Home page

Goodies

Food: 2nd page: Not everyone has the time to bake for the holidays. Our assortment of goodies is sure to please even the pickiest of eaters. This is a feast worthy of a king and fit for a large crowd of people. There will even be a couple of food trucks in the parking lot to grab a lunch while you are shopping for gifts. Don’t forget all those office Christmas parties you will need to bring items for. You are sure to be the hit of the party by grabbing some of our tasty goodies. Contact Information: 2nd page: Calling all artist and local bakers. If you are interested in getting a vendor booth at our fair please contact Sarah Mikos at 623-993-5615. You can even text your interest to that number if you would like. Please send me the details about what you are selling and what type of space you are looking for. Each spot will cost $50. And 5 percent of your sales after the two days. We don’t accept personal check you will need to pay with a credit card or money order. Spots are limited so please call as soon as possible. Payment options: 2nd page: All vendors have agreed to accept credit cards and cash. Sorry we will not allow personal checks of any kind. There are several banks in the area with ATMs if you need to pull out cash. Talk with all venders and make sure they can take credit cards. Remind them that we aren’t allowing personal checks. There are too many chances of not getting paid with this form of payment.
